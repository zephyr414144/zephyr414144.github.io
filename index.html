About Launchpad

Launchpad is a student-focused platform where teenagers (13–18) can offer and find services in their local community. It connects students who need help with students who have skills like tutoring, video editing, photography, gaming coaching, and more.

Key Features

📍 Local Matching
Find nearby students offering the help you need instantly.
🧑‍🎓 Student Marketplace
Offer or request services like tutoring, tech help, or content creation.
⭐ Ratings & Trust
Build reputation through reviews and verified profiles.
💳 Secure Payments
Safe in-app transactions with a small platform fee.

Services You Can Offer

• Academic Tutoring
• Video Editing
• Photography
• Tech Support
• Gaming Coaching
• Social Media Content Creation

Our Vision

Launchpad empowers students to earn money, build real-world skills, and learn entrepreneurship early. It creates a safe, local ecosystem where teenage talent becomes opportunity.
